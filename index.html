Welcome to the document of the crystalmapping. The package is a toolbox for the data collection, processing, analysis and visualization in the crystal mapping. The crystal mapping is a technique to reveal the spatial or oriental distribution of single crystals using a series of x-ray diffraction data. There are an old algorithm and a new algorithm to do this. The package includes both of them. It is recommended to use the new one.
